Diet

Providing the proper food for your pet is important. Here is a table with the diet of my different pets.
Sometimes the food will vary to provide a varied diet which is helpful for the health of the animal.

Animal Feed
Snakes Live rats: Size depends on snake size
Leopard Geckos Rat pups, mealworms and crickets
Tarantula Rat pups, mealworms and crickets
Bearded Dragon Rat pups, dubia roaches and crickets
